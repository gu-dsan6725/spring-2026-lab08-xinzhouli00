### Negotiation Master Script (Works for Any Bill)
> 1. *"I've been a loyal customer since [year]..."* (Gratitude)
> 2. *"...but my bill is higher than competitor rates."* (Problem)
> 3. *"[Competitor] is offering [specific price] for the same service."* (Leverage)
> 4. *"Can you match that, or what can you offer?"* (Ask)
> 5. If no: *"Can I speak to your retention department?"* (Escalate)

### Magic Phrases
- *"Can you escalate me to your retention department?"*
